SCREEN NAME: Standard Reports
MODULE: Reporting
URL PATTERN: /Reporting/MyReports

PURPOSE:
This screen serves as the main hub for accessing all standard reporting categories within the Eyefinity practice management system. It provides organized access to pre-built reports across different operational areas of an eye care practice.

KEY FEATURES:
- Accounting Reports section for financial reporting
- Appointments Reports for scheduling analytics
- Marketing Reports for promotional campaign tracking
- Patient Reports for patient data analysis
- Sales Reports for revenue and transaction tracking
- Inventory Reports for stock management analysis
- Dispensary Reports for optical dispensing metrics
- Revenue understanding promotional banner
- Organized categorization of report types
- Quick List access panel

COMMON USER TASKS:
1. Access accounting reports for financial analysis
2. Generate patient reports for demographics and visit history
3. View sales reports to track revenue performance
4. Run inventory reports to monitor stock levels
5. Create appointment reports for scheduling efficiency
6. Generate dispensary reports for optical operations
7. Access marketing reports to evaluate campaign effectiveness

STEP-BY-STEP: Access Patient Reports
1. Navigate to Reporting > Standard Reports
2. Click on "Patient Reports" from the available categories
3. Select the specific patient report type needed
4. Configure report parameters and run the report

NAVIGATION:
- How to get here: Main menu > Reporting > Standard Reports
- Accessible from here: Individual report categories (Accounting, Appointments, Marketing, Patient, Sales, Inventory, Dispensary Reports), Quick List panel

TIPS AND NOTES:
- Reports are organized by functional area to help users quickly find relevant reporting tools
- The "Understand your actual revenue!" banner suggests revenue cycle management features are available
- Each report category contains multiple specialized reports for that operational area
- Standard reports are pre-configured templates that can typically be customized with date ranges and filters
